About Our Department

Who We Are

The Computer Engineering Department provides high-quality technical education in programming, database systems, networking, artificial intelligence, and modern software technologies. Our mission is to prepare students for successful careers in the IT industry.

Our Mission

To develop innovative engineers with strong technical, analytical, and problem-solving skills who can contribute to society and industry.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer engineering education and research.
